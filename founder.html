The Founder

Mountainthyme Press began with a quiet wish: to hold onto curiosity and make more space for children to find adventure.

It grew from memories of being outdoors, leading the way, making stories with friends, and wanting children to have less noise and more wonder.

The heart of the press is simple: stories that feel warm, kind, adventurous and a little bit magical.
