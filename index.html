Welcome, wanderer

Mountainthyme Press is a small independent children’s press inspired by wild places, kind hearts, and the little moments that make children stop, wonder, and ask, “What’s over there?”

Our stories are made for curious children, tired grown-ups, and anyone who still believes adventure can begin in the grass outside the front door.

Page Two

The Founder

Mountainthyme Press began with a longing to slow down, be curious again, and create something that helps children feel the quiet magic of nature, friendship, kindness, and adventure.

It is rooted in the belief that children need space to explore, imagine, lead, laugh, and discover the world with muddy shoes and open hearts.
